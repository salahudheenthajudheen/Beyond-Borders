# PS-01: Autonomous Attendance and Engagement Monitoring for Large University Classrooms

> **Company:** Netrasemi
> **Vertical:** Smart Campus AI
> **Technology:** Edge AI Vision Chip + Classroom Camera Array

---

## Problem Statement

India has 1,000+ universities and 40,000+ colleges enrolling 43 million students. In large-enrollment courses (100–500 students per classroom), attendance tracking is manual, time-consuming, and easily gamed — proxy attendance is near-universal. Faculty spend 10–15 minutes per class on roll call; students sign for absent peers. More critically, there is no real-time visibility into student engagement — whether students are paying attention, sleeping, or absent — that would enable instructors to adapt their teaching dynamically.

Existing biometric attendance systems (fingerprint, RFID) require physical contact and are slow in large classrooms. Facial recognition systems raise serious privacy concerns and require cloud infrastructure.

---

## Who Faces This Problem

- Faculty in large-enrollment undergraduate courses

## Why the Problem Matters

- Studies show attendance correlates with academic outcomes — but enforcement without automation is impractical at scale
- Faculty time on attendance = 600+ hours per year per large-enrollment professor
- NAAC and NBA accreditation explicitly evaluates attendance monitoring systems
- Student engagement data could transform how lectures are designed and delivered

---

## Proposed Solution

A classroom edge AI system — Netrasemi chip + wide-angle camera array — mounted at the front and back of lecture halls. The system takes a single-pass attendance capture at the start of class (seat occupancy + anonymous student count), and provides continuous engagement analytics to the faculty dashboard: attention levels, zone-wise engagement, drowsiness detection. All processing happens on the edge device; no student images are stored or transmitted. Faculty see a dashboard. Students receive an automated attendance SMS.

**Key features:**
- Seat-level occupancy detection (not face recognition) for privacy preservation
- Head pose and engagement estimation (attentive, looking away, head down)
- Faculty dashboard: real-time engagement heatmap by classroom zone
- Automated attendance notification to students and administration
- No face storage: only anonymized pose and occupancy data retained
- Works in variable classroom lighting, including dim projection environments
- Fully offline: all processing on Netrasemi chip; only aggregate reports transmitted

---

## Why Edge AI Is Needed

- University networks are congested — streaming classroom video to cloud is impractical
- Student video data is extremely privacy-sensitive — off-campus cloud processing is legally and ethically problematic
- Real-time engagement feedback must update during the lecture, not the next day
- Affordable per-classroom deployment requires low-cost edge hardware, not a cloud GPU subscription
