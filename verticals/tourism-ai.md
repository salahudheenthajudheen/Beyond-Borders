# Vertical: Tourism AI

> *Intelligent experiences — for every visitor, at every destination.*

---

## Overview

Tourism AI applies intelligence to visitor experience, site management, safety, and environmental conservation. India's tourism sector generates $250B annually and encompasses heritage sites, wildlife sanctuaries, religious pilgrimage destinations, and natural landscapes. Most of these destinations have poor connectivity — making Edge AI the only viable path to intelligent visitor services.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Heritage Interpretation** — Offline multilingual AI guides at remote sites
**Wildlife Monitoring** — Anti-poaching intelligence and species tracking
**Crowd Safety** — Real-time density monitoring at mass gatherings
**Visitor Analytics** — Non-invasive visitor behavior understanding
**Environmental Impact** — Carrying capacity monitoring and visitor flow management
**Accessibility** — AI-assisted navigation for differently-abled visitors
