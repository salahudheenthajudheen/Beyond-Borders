# Vertical: Robotics AI

> *Machines that work in the real world — India's real world.*

---

## Overview

Robotics AI combines physical systems with on-device intelligence to automate tasks in agriculture, manufacturing, infrastructure, healthcare, and logistics. Indian robotics faces a unique design challenge: the environments where automation is most needed — farms, sewers, construction sites, narrow urban streets — are unstructured, unpredictable, and entirely unlike the clean environments that existing robot platforms are designed for. Edge AI is the enabling layer.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Agricultural Robotics** — Weeding, harvesting, spraying in field conditions
**Infrastructure Inspection** — Sewer, pipeline, and structural inspection robots
**Last-Mile Logistics** — Urban delivery robots for India's dense neighborhoods
**Construction Automation** — Bricklaying, inspection, and materials handling
**Healthcare Robotics** — Assistive and rehabilitation devices
**Search and Rescue** — Autonomous robots for disaster response
