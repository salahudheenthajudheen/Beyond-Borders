# Recommended Reading

> Curated resources for builders, researchers, and founders in the Beyond Borders ecosystem.

---

## Edge AI and Embedded Machine Learning

- **TinyML: Machine Learning with TensorFlow Lite on Arduino and Ultra-Low-Power Microcontrollers** — Pete Warden & Daniel Situnayake — The foundational book for Edge AI development
- **Efficient Deep Learning** — Gaurav Menghani (Google) — Methods for compressing and accelerating neural networks for edge deployment
- [Edge AI and Vision Alliance](https://www.edge-ai-and-vision.com/) — Industry consortium with technical resources
- [TinyML Foundation](https://www.tinyml.org/) — Community, events, and research resources

---

## Computer Vision

- **Deep Learning for Vision Systems** — Mohamed Elgendy — Practical guide to building CV systems
- [Papers With Code — Computer Vision](https://paperswithcode.com/area/computer-vision) — Latest research benchmarks and implementations
- [OpenCV Documentation](https://docs.opencv.org/) — The standard library for computer vision

---

## Agriculture AI

- **Precision Agriculture for Sustainability** — FAO — Policy and technology frameworks
- [PlantVillage](https://plantvillage.psu.edu/) — Plant disease detection platform and dataset
- [AgriProtect Research](https://www.iarc.fr/) — Pesticide use and alternatives
- [ICAR Publications](https://icar.org.in/content/publications) — India agricultural research

---

## Healthcare AI

- **Deep Medicine: How Artificial Intelligence Can Make Healthcare Human Again** — Eric Topol — Vision for AI in healthcare
- [PhysioNet](https://physionet.org/) — Open access clinical and physiological datasets
- [WHO Digital Health guidelines](https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240029200) — Global standards for AI in health

---

## India-Specific Context

- **India's Trillion Dollar Opportunity** — McKinsey Global Institute — Technology's economic potential in India
- [India Semiconductor Mission](https://www.india.gov.in/semiconductor-mission) — India's chip manufacturing strategy
- [NASSCOM AI Adoption Report](https://nasscom.in/knowledge-center/publications) — AI adoption across Indian industries
- [Digital India Reports](https://digitalindia.gov.in/di_report) — Government technology programs

---

## Startup and Innovation

- **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries — The foundational startup methodology
- **Zero to One** — Peter Thiel — How to build genuinely new things
- **Crossing the Chasm** — Geoffrey Moore — How technology products reach mainstream markets
- **The Innovator's Dilemma** — Clayton Christensen — How industries are disrupted

## Disaster Management and Resilience

- [NDMA Guidelines](https://ndma.gov.in/guidelines) — India National Disaster Management Authority technical standards
- [UNDRR Sendai Framework](https://www.undrr.org/publication/sendai-framework-disaster-risk-reduction-2015-2030) — Global disaster risk reduction framework
- [Global Early Warning Systems](https://www.wmo.int/pages/prog/drr/projects/Earlywarn/earlywarning_en.html) — WMO resources on early warning
