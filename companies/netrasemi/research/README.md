# Netrasemi — Research Directions

This folder documents open research questions and directions relevant to Netrasemi's Edge AI technology and its applications.

---

## Open Research Questions

### Chip Architecture
- What neural network architectures are optimal for Netrasemi's specific silicon constraints?
- How do we minimize inference latency for multi-model pipelines on a single chip?

### Computer Vision
- How do we build robust crop disease recognition models for Indian agricultural conditions?
- What are the best approaches for low-light and adverse-weather vision in maritime environments?

### Systems and Deployment
- How do we design self-healing edge AI systems that degrade gracefully when models drift?
- What are the best OTA (over-the-air) update protocols for edge devices in low-bandwidth environments?

### Data and Privacy
- How do we create large-scale, high-quality labeled datasets for Indian agricultural, healthcare, and industrial domains?
- What privacy-preserving federated learning approaches work on resource-constrained chips?
