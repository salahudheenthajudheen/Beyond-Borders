# Vertical: Maritime AI

> *Intelligent oceans — safer ships, smarter ports, sustainable seas.*

---

## Overview

Maritime AI applies intelligence to vessel navigation, port operations, underwater infrastructure, fisheries management, and marine environmental monitoring. India's 7,500km coastline, 12 major ports, and $9B Sagarmala program make maritime AI a strategic national priority. Edge AI is essential because maritime environments — at sea, underwater, and in remote port approaches — have no cloud connectivity.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Vessel Safety** — Collision avoidance and traffic intelligence
**Port Efficiency** — Berth optimization and cargo handling
**Fisheries Protection** — IUU fishing detection and marine reserve monitoring
**Infrastructure Inspection** — Underwater hull, jetty, and cable inspection
**Marine Environmental Monitoring** — Water quality, pollution, and ecosystem health
**Offshore Operations** — Autonomous inspection and maintenance of offshore assets
