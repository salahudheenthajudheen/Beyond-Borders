# PS-01: Real-Time Visual Defect Detection on High-Speed Production Lines

> **Company:** Netrasemi
> **Vertical:** Smart Manufacturing AI
> **Technology:** Edge AI Vision Chip + Inline Camera Array

---

## Problem Statement

In India's manufacturing sector — auto components, electronics, textiles, pharmaceuticals — quality control still relies primarily on human inspectors watching production lines that run at 100–300 units per minute. Human attention degrades after 20 minutes of continuous monitoring; inspectors miss 20–30% of defects even under normal conditions. Defective products that slip through generate warranty claims, production line stoppages, export rejections, and customer churn. The cost of defective product reaching the field is typically 10–100x the cost of catching it at the line.

Existing machine vision systems from German and Japanese vendors cost $200,000–$500,000 per inspection station — viable only for the largest plants, not for India's vast ecosystem of Tier 2 and Tier 3 suppliers.

---

- Auto component manufacturers supplying OEMs (Maruti, Tata, Mahindra)
- Electronics contract manufacturers

---

## Why the Problem Matters

- India's manufacturing sector targets $1 trillion output by 2025; quality gaps threaten that ambition
- Defect rates above 500 PPM (parts per million) cause OEM supplier disqualification
- Export rejections cost India's manufacturing exporters $4B+ annually
- SME manufacturers cannot access quality automation at current price points

---

## Proposed Solution

A modular edge AI visual inspection unit — Netrasemi chip + high-resolution line scan camera + structured LED lighting — priced at ₹3–5 lakh ($4,000–6,000), installable on any production line within a day. The unit runs domain-specific defect detection models (scratches, cracks, burrs, dimensional variance, color inconsistency, missing components) at production line speed. Defective units are flagged and automatically rejected by a pneumatic ejector. All inspection events are logged with images for quality audit.

**Key features:**
- 300+ inspections per minute at full line speed
- < 0.1% false rejection rate target
- Easy model retraining for new product SKUs (no ML expertise needed)
- Real-time OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) dashboard
- Integrates with MES (Manufacturing Execution System) via OPC-UA or Modbus

---

## Why Edge AI Is Needed

- Production lines cannot tolerate cloud latency — a 50ms decision window at 300 units/min
- Manufacturing images (proprietary product designs, quality specs) must stay within the plant
- Factory floor networks are isolated OT (operational technology) environments with no internet
- Low-power, compact form factor required to mount inline without disrupting line layout

---

## Feasibility

Visual inspection AI is a mature field. YOLOv8, EfficientDet, and anomaly detection approaches (PatchCore, FastFlow) all achieve production-ready accuracy. Netrasemi chips can run these at line speed. The challenge is: building a product that is easy to retrain for new product types without an ML engineer on-site, and achieving the pricing target that makes it accessible to SME manufacturers.

**Feasibility Score: 8/10**
