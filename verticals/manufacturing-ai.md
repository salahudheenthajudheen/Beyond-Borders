# Vertical: Smart Manufacturing AI

> *Making India's factories intelligent, safe, and globally competitive.*

---

## Overview

Smart Manufacturing AI applies intelligence to production processes, quality control, predictive maintenance, worker safety, and supply chain management. India's manufacturing sector — 40B today, targeting T by 2025 — cannot scale without dramatically improving productivity and quality. Edge AI is the key enabler because manufacturing environments are air-gapped OT networks where cloud connectivity is unavailable, real-time decisions are required, and proprietary process data must stay on-premise.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Defect Detection** — Zero-defect manufacturing through real-time visual inspection
**Predictive Maintenance** — Eliminating unplanned downtime across machine fleets
**Worker Safety** — Real-time PPE and hazard monitoring
**Energy Optimization** — Intelligent energy management at the machine level
**Process Control** — Closed-loop AI-driven process optimization
**Inventory Intelligence** — Real-time parts tracking and consumption prediction
