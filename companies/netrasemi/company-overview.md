# Netrasemi — Company Overview

> **Headquarters:** India
> **Focus:** Edge AI Semiconductor
> **Stage:** Deep Tech / Product Development
> **Website:** [netrasemi.com](https://netrasemi.com)

---

## Mission

To build the silicon backbone for intelligent edge systems — enabling AI-powered decisions that are fast, private, and resilient, without dependence on cloud infrastructure.

---

## What They Do

Netrasemi designs and builds Edge AI chips — specialized semiconductors that run AI inference workloads directly on the device. Their chips power systems that need to see, understand, and act in real time, in environments where cloud connectivity is unreliable, latency is unacceptable, or data privacy is non-negotiable.

Unlike general-purpose processors or cloud AI APIs, Netrasemi's chips are purpose-built for edge intelligence: optimized for low power, high throughput on vision and sensor data, and tight integration with embedded systems.

---

## Why India?

India represents a unique inflection point in the global semiconductor landscape. With the government's push under the India Semiconductor Mission, a rapidly growing pool of chip design talent, and enormous domestic demand for intelligent systems in agriculture, healthcare, manufacturing, and defense, Netrasemi is positioned at the center of a strategic national and global opportunity.

India's scale of problems — hundreds of millions of smallholder farmers, a massive rural healthcare gap, one of the world's largest manufacturing bases — is precisely the kind of environment where Edge AI is not a luxury but a necessity.

---

## Key Capabilities

- **On-Device AI Inference** — Run trained models directly on the chip, no internet required
- **Computer Vision** — Real-time image and video analysis at the edge
- **Multi-Sensor Fusion** — Integrate data from cameras, LiDAR, thermal sensors, accelerometers, and more
- **Low Power Operation** — Deploy in battery-powered or solar-powered field devices
- **Privacy-Preserving AI** — Sensitive data never leaves the device
- **Offline Intelligence** — Full functionality in areas with no connectivity

## Relevance to Beyond Borders

Netrasemi's technology sits at the intersection of several of the world's most pressing challenge domains:
