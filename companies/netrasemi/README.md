# Netrasemi — Edge AI for the Real World

> *"Intelligence at the edge. Decisions in real time."*

**Netrasemi** is an Indian semiconductor company building Edge AI chips that bring on-device intelligence to the physical world — in fields, hospitals, factories, ports, campuses, and disaster zones.

---

## Why Netrasemi?

Most AI today lives in the cloud. It requires connectivity, tolerates latency, and exposes data to third parties. For the problems that matter most — a farmer detecting crop disease at dawn, a doctor monitoring a patient in a rural clinic, a factory machine predicting failure mid-shift — cloud AI is too slow, too expensive, and too fragile.

Netrasemi's Edge AI chips change the equation. They bring real-time intelligence to the device itself, enabling AI that works without internet, responds in milliseconds, costs less to operate, and keeps sensitive data private.
