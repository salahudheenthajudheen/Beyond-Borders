# Vertical: Smart Campus AI

> *Campuses that know their students — and keep them safe.*

---

## Overview

Smart Campus AI applies intelligence to learning environments, student safety, accessibility, energy management, and campus operations. India's 1,000+ universities and 40,000+ colleges serve 43 million students — but most campuses run on manual systems for attendance, security, and facility management. Edge AI enables a new generation of privacy-respecting, offline-capable campus intelligence.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Learning Analytics** — Real-time engagement and attention monitoring
**Campus Safety** — Behavioral anomaly detection and incident prevention
**Accessibility** — AI-assisted navigation and learning for differently-abled students
**Energy Management** — Intelligent HVAC, lighting, and utility optimization
**Facility Intelligence** — Predictive maintenance and space utilization
**Exam Integrity** — Privacy-preserving proctoring without cloud surveillance
