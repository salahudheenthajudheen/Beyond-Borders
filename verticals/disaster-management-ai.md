# Vertical: Disaster Management AI

> *Intelligence that saves lives — before, during, and after disaster.*

---

## Overview

Disaster Management AI applies intelligence to early warning systems, emergency response, search and rescue, and infrastructure resilience. India is one of the world's most disaster-prone countries — floods, cyclones, earthquakes, and forest fires affect millions annually. Edge AI is life-critical here because disasters destroy the very infrastructure that cloud-dependent systems require.

---

## Core Problem Areas

**Early Warning** — Flash flood, earthquake, and cyclone prediction at the community level
**Search and Rescue** — Survivor detection and rescue optimization
**Damage Assessment** — Rapid post-disaster structural and infrastructure assessment
**Forest Fire** — Early detection and spread prediction
**Evacuation Intelligence** — Real-time crowd and traffic management during evacuations
**Resilient Communication** — AI-enabled mesh networks when infrastructure fails
